Week 5: Standards in Curation

Task 1 - Creating a Schema for my MEI File


For this weeks tasks, we were to create a metadata schema based on our group work from the Tuesday session.

My group had a large variety of music pieces, from classical music to electronic dance music, so the metadata we selected was really broad. Below are some of the metadata elements I've selected which relate to my piece:

  • Title: The name of my selected piece
  • Composer: The name of the person who created the orignal work(s).
  • Arranger: The name of the person who arranged this version of the orignal work(s).
  • Encoder: The name of the person who encoded the piece of music.
  • Date: The date the encoded file was published.
  • Publisher: The name of the person who published the file.
  • Publisher Location: The geographical place where the file was published.
  • Key: The key of the piece of music.
  • Tempo: The tempo of the transcribed piece of music.
  • Performance Medium: The instruments the music is played on.
